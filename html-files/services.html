An introductory session where you, the pet owner, meets with me in person to discuss your pet's needs, behavior, and any requirements. This ensures a safe environment for both me and your dog.

Comprehensive care provided for pets in the comfort of their own home. This service includes feeding, exercise, and companionship, ensuring peace of mind for you as the owner while you are away. Pricing: £50 for one dog, £75 for two dogs. Pet sitting is only available on weekends.

Specialised service where I will ensure the comfort and well-being of your pet during weddings, allowing you to enjoy your celebrations without worrying about your dog.
